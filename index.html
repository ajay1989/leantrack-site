Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Nine focused tools built for real fitness and fat loss progress.

🏋️

Workout Planner

Build a weekly training schedule with strength and cardio days. Add exercises, set targets, and track every session live — sets, reps, and weight included.

📋

Live Workout Logging

Log sets in real time with smart pre-fill from your last session. Add or remove sets on the fly, and your progress is auto-saved as you go.

🏃

Cardio Logging

Log treadmill, cycling, rowing, elliptical, cross trainer, stepper, swimming, and more — with duration and distance tracking.

⚖️

Weight Tracking

Log daily weight with progress charts, weekly trends, and smart analysis of your fat loss pace.

📊

Body Composition

US Navy formula calculates body fat %, lean mass, and tracks how your composition changes over time.

🎯

Fat Loss Planner

Calorie targets, body fat milestones with target body weight at each level, and timeline estimates — from Normal to Athletic to Visible Abs.

🥗

Nutrition Targets

Personalized calorie and macro targets based on your body stats, with a protein food browser.

🧠

Daily Insights

Smart daily snapshot highlights what's going well and where you can improve. No AI subscription, no paywalls.

🏆

Streaks & Achievements

Stay motivated with daily streaks, fitness levels, and milestone badges that unlock as you progress.

🔒

Your data never leaves your phone

LeanTrack™ stores everything locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData. No accounts, no cloud sync, no data collection. Your health data is yours alone.
